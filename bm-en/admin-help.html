Frequently Asked Questions
Can I set a menu price lower than the HQ standard price? ▾
Yes, you can freely adjust prices based on your branch's situation. However, HQ policy may set a minimum price threshold.
How do I configure the shot add-on policy? ▾
On the menu settings page, you can set the add-on shot price and maximum number of shots for each menu item.
Can I create membership products myself? ▾
Yes, under Members & Marketing > Members, you can check the membership status and freely create products as subscription, cup pass, or pass types.
Can a branch change the ad videos? ▾
Ad videos are managed under Members & Marketing > Ad Videos. To maintain brand consistency, they are configured with HQ admin permissions.
Where do I manage inventory? ▾
You can check your branch's stock status under Branch Management > Inventory.
